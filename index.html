How Defendly Works?

Initiate Scan:

Enter the website URL and click the scan button to start the security assessment.

Backend Processing:

Defendly sends a request to our backend infrastructure, extracting network payloads from the target website.

ML-Based Analysis:

Our sophisticated ML algorithms analyze the extracted data, segregating headers based on their contextual importance.

Generate Security Score:

The analysis culminates in a comprehensive security score and detailed report, highlighting potential vulnerabilities and providing remediation steps.

FAQ

Got questions? Dive into our FAQs to find quick answers about Defendly’s AI-driven cybersecurity solutions.

  • What is Defendly?

    Defendly is a cutting-edge cybersecurity tool designed by Bug Hunters. It provides real-time security scans for websites, helping to detect and mitigate vulnerabilities instantly.

  • How does Defendly work?

    Defendly integrates with your Chrome browser as an extension. It continuously monitors your website’s interactions over the internet, identifying potential threats and providing actionable insights to improve security.

  • Who should use Defendly?

    Defendly is ideal for businesses, developers, freelancers, and educational institutions looking to secure their online presence. Whether you're managing an e-commerce platform, a small business website, or a personal project, Defendly offers robust protection.

  • Is Defendly easy to install?

    Yes, installing Defendly is quick and simple. Just visit the Chrome Web Store, search for Defendly, and click “Add to Chrome.” Follow the on-screen instructions, and you’ll be up and running in no time.

  • What kind of threats can Defendly detect?

    Defendly can detect a wide range of cyber threats, including malware, phishing attempts, unauthorized access, and other vulnerabilities that could compromise your website’s security.
